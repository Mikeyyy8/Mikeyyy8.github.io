## Introduction
Welcome to Propound, the Word of the Day mobile application. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, including children. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your personal information when you use the Propound app.

By using Propound, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Do Not Collect
Propound is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from our users, including:

- Names, email addresses, or contact details
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Any other personally identifiable information

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
Propound is accessible to users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 15 U.S.C. § 6501 et seq., and the FTC's COPPA Rule:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
- We do not require account registration or sign-in to use the app.
- We do not display behaviorally targeted advertising.
- We do not include social features that allow children to communicate with others.

Parents and guardians may contact us at any time with questions or concerns about their child's use of Propound.

## How the App Works
Propound provides a daily word, its definition, usage examples, and etymology directly within the app. All content is delivered through the app itself. No internet connection is required to access previously loaded content, and no personal data is transmitted in connection with your use of the app.

## Third-Party Services
Propound does not integrate third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media SDKs that collect user data.

## Data Security
Because we do not collect any personal data, there is no user data stored on our servers. The app operates entirely on your device.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Because Propound does not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to access, delete, or opt out of selling. We do not sell personal information.

California users may contact us at [privacy@propoundapp.com](mailto:privacy@propoundapp.com) with any questions regarding their privacy rights.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated effective date. Continued use of Propound after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
